This is a proof-of-concept prototype for an application design program.

Click here to go to Design mode, then drag components to the stage or onto a horizontal/vertical list.

A few pointers:

  • Dashed borders turn black when they become a drop target
  • You can drag components on the stage in and out of drop targets
    However, note that there is a bug causing items dropped on anything but lists to disappear
  • Images can be resized
